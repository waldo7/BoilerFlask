About FlaskStuct

FlaskStuct is an opinionated, highly organized project scaffold developed to provide developers with a robust foundation for building production-grade Flask web applications.

We believe web development shouldn't require reinventing the wheel. By combining clean architectural design patterns with state-of-the-art security, session management, and visual styling, FlaskStuct lets you focus strictly on what makes your application unique.

The Architecture Core

FlaskStuct enforces key architectural pillars:

  • App Factory Pattern: Prevents circular imports and maintains modularity.
  • Blueprint Separation: Decouples routes cleanly.
  • Extensions Registry: Isolated initialization inside `extensions.py`.
